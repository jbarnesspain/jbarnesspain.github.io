So, you've decided to get to know me a bit better then? Here's me in a nutshell.

I was always interested in languages, but a undergrad Latin course convinced me that languages were a world that I needed to explore. I studied linguistics and began to learn languages. After spending a year in Germany, I moved to Spain and studied Spanish, Basque and Catalan.

I did my Master's at Universitat Pompeu Fabra in Barcelona on Emotion Detection and now I'm doing my PhD on cross-lingual sentiment analysis for under-resourced languages.
